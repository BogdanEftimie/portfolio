02

Gym Log

A fully customizable gym tracking app. Build your own workout templates with fine-tuned controls: warmup sets, reps, weight, %1RM, RPE, rest and notes. Personalize your home screen and navigation to show exactly what you need. Track workout history on a calendar, log quick sessions, and stay consistent with an activity tracker. No account required, all data is stored locally on your device, with full backup export, restore, and data reset options. Supports dark, dim, and light mode with three font size options.
